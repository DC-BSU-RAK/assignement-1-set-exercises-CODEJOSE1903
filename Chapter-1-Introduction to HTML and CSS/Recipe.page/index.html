Brigadeiro

This is a quick and easy brigadeiro recipe,perfect all occasions

Ingridients

  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 tablespoons of unsweetend cocoa powder
  • 1 table spoon of butter
  • chocolate sprinkles for rolling

Directions

  1. gather all ingridients
  2. combine condensed milk, cocoa powder and butter ia a medium saucepan over medium heat.cook and stir for 10 minutes until it gets thick.
  3. transfer mixture into a shallow bowl and allow it to cool.
  4. pinch off some of the dough and roll it into a small ball than place it on a plate.Repeat the process with the reamining mixture ,then roll each brigadeiro in the chocolate sprinkles.
  5. place the brigadeiros in a refrigireitor and serve when cool.
